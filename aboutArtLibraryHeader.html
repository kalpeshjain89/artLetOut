About Art Library

The concept is different from what you see generally everywhere, either you buy or you rent.Art Library makes this simple similar to your collection of books you can now collect variety of art and rotate it and enjoy new art every time it catches your eye.
How does it work? It works like any other online rental service. You register, pick your Art and they will delivered to your doorstep. You can queue them up if the Art is in circulation. You can keep a maximum of Art as per the plan you purchase. The total number of Art per month varies depending upon your plan.

How much does it cost? There is a one-time registration fee as per the plan in rupees and minimal security deposit . These two charges are common across the plans. Based upon the plan you choose the monthly charge varies.
The service is available only in Pune, Mumbai for now for more details you can contact Art Let Out .
